2.1 Regression- Concrete Compressive Strength

2.1.1 Summary

The dataset ‘Concrete_Data.csv’ describes variables that contribute towards different measurements of concrete compressive strength. According to the dataset source -“The concrete compressive strength is a highly nonlinear function of age and ingredients.” It may be fair to assume then that age will have an affect on the compressive stregth along with one or more ingredients. These ingredients include cement, blast furnace slag, fly ash, water, superplasticizer, coarse aggregate, and fine aggregate. Concrete compressive stregnth is the output variable, while the other varaibles (such as ingredients and age) are input variables.

# Regression: Import data for regression question - concrete compressive strength
